Our Story

A vision born
from grief.

Eterna was founded by Pierre Béliveau with a single mission: to find another way to honor the departed. Through years of research and patented technology, he created the Capsule Pyrale — a hermetic funeral container that preserves the body without chemicals, for 60 to 100 years. More than a technical product. A tribute rooted in dignity and peace.

Our Mission

A new way to say goodbye.

To offer a farewell that is softer, more human, and more peaceful. Through unique technology, we preserve the body without decomposition, inside a capsule designed for the future, yet rooted in love.

How we got here

  1. Childhood

    A promise made

    As a boy, Pierre Béliveau lost a childhood friend to drowning. At the funeral, he was struck by the thought that burial also meant contact with rising groundwater. That memory stayed with him for life and became a mission: to find another way to honor the departed.

  2. 2011

    First prototype test

    After years of research into hermetic containment, inert gas technology, and material science, Pierre completes and tests the first Capsule Pyrale prototype in Granby, Québec.

  3. 2014

    Conclusive results

    Research confirms the technology works as intended: inert nitrogen gas preserves the body without chemicals, without odor, without environmental contamination. Patent filed.

  4. Patent

    Patent filed & certified

    The Capsule Pyrale's hermetic preservation technology is formally patented. Certification obtained in Canada and the United States, with additional countries in progress.

  5. Today

    100% Québec. For families everywhere.

    Eterna operates from Granby, Québec, manufacturing every capsule with the same values Pierre set out with: dignity, respect, and a deep care for families navigating the hardest moments of their lives.

Our values

Innovation

Rethinking the farewell.

Rethinking funeral practices with advanced, respectful technology. The Capsule Pyrale was born from a desire to reinvent farewells with dignity, gentleness, and a forward-looking approach.

Respect

Every detail matters.

Respect for the body, the family, and grief. We believe the last gesture we offer to our loved ones should reflect their true worth. No compromises. No shortcuts.

Transparency

Clarity in uncertain times.

Clarity in a time filled with uncertainty. Honest, open approach. We explain everything, we hide nothing, and we guide families with patience and care from first contact to final farewell.

Sustainability

No toxic trace. Ever.

Recyclable, stable, airtight. No chemicals, no embalming fluid, no soil contamination, no groundwater impact. A responsible choice for the family and for the earth.

Kindness

Ease the burden of loss.

Gentle, human, deeply caring. Because saying goodbye is never simple. We work at the family's pace, with no pressure and no countdown clock. Grief deserves calm, not chaos.

Cultural Openness

Every tradition welcomed.

Death has no borders. Every tradition welcomed with sensitivity. The Capsule Pyrale is designed to accommodate every culture, every belief, and every unique vision of a meaningful farewell.
