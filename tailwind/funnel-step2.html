About the Capsule Pyrale

The Capsule Pyrale is an innovative hermetic funeral container made of recyclable aluminum with an ultra-resistant polycarbonate cover. The body is sealed with inert nitrogen gas, which naturally dries the body — preventing decomposition without any chemicals, embalming fluids, or odors. Estimated preservation: 60 to 100 years. 100% Québec manufacturing. Lifetime warranty.

Color

Smooth matte high-end finish. Both options available in full production.

Interior LED Color

Integrated LED strips line the interior of the capsule, creating a soothing, luminous atmosphere.

Engraving

Optional engraving on the capsule exterior. Details finalized with your Eterna advisor.

Handles

Optional integrated handles for moving and placement by funeral home staff.
