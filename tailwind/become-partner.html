Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to carry inventory? +

Not with the rental model. You present the option to families and place an order when they choose the Capsule Pyrale. We handle delivery. The purchase model places a permanent unit at your facility.

What types of cases is it suited for? +

The Capsule Pyrale is suitable for standard cases, as well as bodies that are not viewable due to infection, advanced decomposition, or delays. It is also used for international transport and repatriation, and works in crypts and mausoleums.

Is special training required? +

We provide full training for your team as part of the partnership onboarding. No tools are required and the operation is designed to be managed by a single person. Training is straightforward and included at no extra cost.

How is the capsule delivered to remote regions? +

The Capsule Pyrale is designed to be transportable by a single person and can be delivered directly to your facility on demand. This makes it particularly well-suited for funeral homes operating in remote or isolated communities.

How is pricing structured? +

Pricing details are shared privately upon application review. There is no public pricing — we discuss the right model and terms during our initial call. Contact us at info@pyramidestal.com or 1 844 695-5613 for more information.
