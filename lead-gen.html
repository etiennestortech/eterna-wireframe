Pre-Planning with the Capsule Pyrale

Planning ahead is one of the most generous things you can do for your family. By documenting your wishes now, you spare them from difficult decisions in a moment of grief — and give them more time to gather, remember, and say goodbye. The Capsule Pyrale allows for extended viewing without embalming, in a dignified, luminous setting. Request a quote today to start the conversation.

What the Capsule Pyrale offers
  1. Hermetic aluminum structure — airtight, zero leakage
  2. Inert nitrogen preservation — no chemicals, no embalming
  3. 60 to 100 years of estimated preservation
  4. Transparent polycarbonate cover (aviation-grade)
  5. Integrated LED interior lighting — customizable color
  6. Available in Black or White with smooth matte finish
  7. Optional engraving: name, date, or meaningful word
  8. 100% Québec manufacturing — certified in Canada & USA
  9. Lifetime warranty on structure
