Product Details

Details ›

The Capsule Pyrale is Eterna's flagship product — an innovative hermetic funeral container made of recyclable aluminum, designed and manufactured 100% in Québec. It is an alternative to both traditional burial and cremation.

The body is placed inside, the capsule is sealed with a hermetic key, and inert nitrogen gas is introduced. This naturally dries the body, preventing decomposition without any chemicals, embalming fluids, or odors. The body can be preserved for 60 to 100 years.

The aviation-grade polycarbonate cover allows a clear, luminous view of the interior. Integrated LED lighting can be personalized by color. Optional engraving allows a name, date, or meaningful word to be added to the exterior. Every capsule is serialized for traceability.

Materials ›

Main structure: Recyclable aluminum — lightweight, durable, zero soil contamination.

Cover: Ultra-resistant polycarbonate, aviation-grade and unbreakable. Allows full visibility of the interior.

Sealing joints: High-density industrial rubber — airtight, zero leakage.

Bottom shell: ABS — the same standard used in automotive and medical equipment.

Mechanism: Dual hydraulic system for smooth, assisted opening and closing. No tools required.

Interior lighting: Integrated LED strips, fully customizable color.

Gas: Inert nitrogen — naturally dries the body without chemicals, odor, or toxic release.

How It Works ›

Step 1 — Gentle Placement: The body is placed inside by qualified professionals using a discreet, secure system. Every gesture is slow, deliberate, respectful. No rushed movements.

Step 2 — Hermetic Sealing: The cover is lowered and locked with a special sealing key. The mechanism creates a completely airtight barrier — no leaks, no odors, no infiltration possible.

Step 3 — Inert Gas Preservation: The capsule is filled with inert nitrogen gas. This naturally dries the body, slowing decomposition without chemicals or toxic release. The body can be preserved for up to 100 years.

Sustainability ›

100% recyclable aluminum. No chemicals, no embalming fluid, no toxic release. No soil contamination, no groundwater impact.

The Capsule Pyrale was designed from the ground up to leave no harmful trace — on the body, on the family, or on the environment. It is fully compatible with cemeteries, crypts, mausoleums, and international transport.
